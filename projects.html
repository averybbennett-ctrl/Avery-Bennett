  • Returns to Graduate Education Paper

    • Examined Efficiency Wage, Neoclassical, and Post-Keynesian Institutional theories.
    • Used empirical data to assess employment, poverty reduction, and business performance.
    • Analyzed trade-offs between worker protection and economic impact.
    • Recommended indexed wage increases and targeted support for small businesses.
